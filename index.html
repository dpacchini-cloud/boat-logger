Calendar Sharing

Two one-way feeds
Logger is the source of truth. Two .ics files publish to your shared Google/Apple calendars: a full record for you, and a privacy-safe blocked-days feed for your assistant.
For You / Accountant ⬇
Full calendar — every entry with category, hours, description. Use for personal calendar overlay or tax records.
For Assistant / Shared Calendar ⬇
Maintenance + Personal days only, labeled "Boat Unavailable" (no detail revealed). Assistant subscribes to this and sees blocked days when scheduling charters.
Setup once: Upload to a stable URL (iCloud Drive shared link, GitHub Gist, Dropbox), then have your assistant subscribe by URL in Google Calendar. Re-upload after each session to refresh.
